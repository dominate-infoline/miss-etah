About the Organizer

Dominate Infoline Pvt. Ltd.

Based in Etah, Uttar Pradesh, Dominate Infoline Pvt. Ltd. has spent close to a decade bringing the district together through pageantry, fashion and community celebration. Miss Etah is our flagship event — a stage where local talent is groomed, celebrated and given a platform to shine, held each year as part of the district's own Etah Mahotsav.

"Let's come together for success."

Every edition combines professional grooming workshops, a ramp walk, talent rounds and a jury of respected names from fashion, media and academia — culminating in a grand finale at the Etah Exhibition Ground.

About the Magazine

Dominate Infoline Pvt. Ltd.

Dominate Media Magazine by Dominate Infoline Pvt. Ltd. is a premium publication dedicated to celebrating beauty, talent, fashion, and inspiring success stories. The magazine highlights the journeys of Miss Etah, Miss Teen Etah Queen, fashion icons, rising models, entrepreneurs, and creative professionals, giving them a platform to share their achievements, struggles, and success. Featuring exclusive interviews, winner spotlights, fashion trends, beauty insights, and industry stories, Dominate Media Magazine serves as a powerful voice for emerging talent and a source of inspiration for the youth. It is designed to recognize excellence, promote individuality, and connect the worlds of fashion, beauty, and business.

“Where Beauty Meets Talent, and Success Finds Its Spotlight.”

The magazine also provides excellent advertising opportunities for brands, businesses, beauty academies, fashion studios, makeup artists, salons, and local enterprises to showcase their products and services to a growing audience. Through both print and digital reach, advertisers can expand their visibility, strengthen their brand presence, and connect directly with potential customers. Dominate Media Magazine is more than just a magazine — it is a platform where talent meets opportunity, success gets recognized, and businesses find a space to grow.
